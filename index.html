Commercial Control for Edinburgh's Construction Sector.

Operating a construction business in Edinburgh comes with unique pressures. Between navigating strict local authority planning, managing constrained city-centre sites, and dealing with volatile material costs, you are busy enough delivering the physical build.

But operational success does not guarantee financial success. Builders often leave significant profit on site due to a lack of rigorous commercial management.

Builders QS acts as your dedicated, outsourced commercial department. We bridge the gap between site delivery and financial return. Whether you are a main contractor working on a New Town renovation or a subcontractor scaling up across the Lothians, we provide the strategic quantity surveying services required to protect your bottom line.

Underpriced Tenders

Winning work is useless if the job runs at a loss. Relying on outdated rates in a fluctuating Scottish market is dangerous. We provide highly accurate, analytical estimating services so you price work to win, and to profit.

Missed Variations

Scope creep is the silent killer of contractor margins. If you are completing extra work on site without formal change control and agreed pricing, you are essentially funding the client's build. We implement strict variation capture.

Weak Cash Flow

Late payments and disputed valuations choke your ability to operate. We submit watertight, professionally structured valuations and applications for payment, ensuring money moves into your account on time.

The Commercial Weight of a Full-Time QS, Without the Overhead.

Hiring a full-time, experienced Quantity Surveyor in Edinburgh is expensive. For many small to mid-sized builders, it represents an unnecessary overhead. Builders QS provides a flexible alternative. You get access to senior-level commercial acumen exactly when you need it—whether that is pricing a one-off competitive tender, managing the monthly valuations on a live project, or aggressively closing out a disputed final account.
